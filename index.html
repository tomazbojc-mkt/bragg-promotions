Terms & frequently asked

Who can enter?

Entry is by invitation only, at the Operator Group level. Any and all cages connected to an invited Operator Group may take part and contribute to that group's points total. Participating Studios are Atomic Slot Lab, Bluberi, Four Leaf Gaming, Indigo Magic, King Show Games, Oryx, Rapid Play, Reflex Gaming and Wild Streak Gaming.

What are the minimum requirements to qualify?

Have all participating games live on each participating cage on or before 1 September 2026; complete at least one Real Estate milestone; and document all completed Real Estate and Player Engagement milestones with screenshots.

How are ties broken?

Matching point totals are resolved in order: (1) highest turnover share of participating titles vs. total portfolio turnover for the Participating Studios in September 2026; (2) points total for completed Real Estate milestones; (3) combined Growth milestone points.

How and when are prizes awarded?

The top 3 operator groups by points are contacted in the first business week of October 2026 to plan reward allocation. Trip/experience details are agreed during October 2026. The custom game delivery timeline follows Bragg's standard development frameworks, asset templates, local regulatory approval and studio capacity. Promotional budget components must be planned by the stated November deadlines or are forfeited, and are deducted from the January 2027 invoice covering December 2026.

Full campaign terms & conditions

The 50K September Slam Campaign runs from 00:00:01 CET on Tuesday 1 September 2026 until 23:59:59 CET on Wednesday 30 September 2026. The baseline for all Growth milestone calculations is the full month of May 2026.

For any Real Estate or Player Engagement milestone to count, it must be documented (one screenshot per game positioned) either to the shared folder or sent to your Bragg Account Manager on or before 5PM CET on 30 September 2026. Each screenshot must show the current date and website URL, and each file name must include the website name.
